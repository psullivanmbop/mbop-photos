# About the Moapa Band of Paiutes Historical Photograph Collection

This collection is comprised of historic photographs owned and stewarded by the Moapa Band of Paiutes. Its purpose is to document the vibrant history of the tribe in visual form. It is a work in progress.

## Background and Collection Management

Established in 2026, the collection is a product of the Moapa Band of Paiutes Archival Department (founded in the same year) as a means of publishing culturally significant images identified during processing of the tribe's large document collection. It is managed by the Tribal Archivist.

## Privacy Protections

To protect the privacy of individuals represented in photographs, the Tribal Archivist has removed names and other identifying information from images and descriptions of photographs in the tribe's original document collection, with the exception of information about public figures (e.g., Nevada Senator Howard Cannon). The Tribal Archivist will form an agreement with donors concerning privacy policies.

## Donations

To donate a digital or physical photograph, please contact the Tribal Archivist by email at: psullivan@moapabandofpaiutes.org. Materials should pertain to the Moapa Band of Paiutes and be historical (20 years or older) in origin.

## Rights

All rights of images in the collection belong to the Moapa Band of Paiutes. Do not use or republish materials without permission of the tribe. To discuss rights, use, and republication, contact the Tribal Archivist.

## Contact Information

All questions, comments, and suggestions about the collection should be referred to the Tribal Archivist: psullivan@moapabandofpaiutes.org
